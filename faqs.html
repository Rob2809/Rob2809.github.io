Frequently asked questions

Why is my computer slow?

There are many reasons why your computer can be performing poorly. These range from old and absolete hardware, to inssuficient memory or malicious software. If you feel your PC is performing poorly please contact me to troubleshoot.

How do I achieve best performance?

Your computers performance should always be tailored to your needs, however if you wish to improve it you should look at upgrading hardware such as RAM, Solid state drives, along with upgraded CPUs or Graphics cards if required. The software you have installed in your machine can also have a big impact on perfermance.

Are repair costs expensive?

The common myth is that PC upgrades & repairs cost a lot of money. This is inaccurate, in reality most repairs and upgrades aren't expensive. Contact me with any cost queries you may have and we can discuss.

What does a PC service consist of?

A general PC service consists firstly of breaking down your machine, performing a physical inspection of all internal hardware. All internals are then meticously cleaned of dust and your machine is re-configured. The next stage is to perform a full software inspection on the operating system to determine what improvements need to be made, removing incorrect software and installing correct software as required. Your machines data is fully backed up before any work so your data is always safe! Your machine will be returned running smoother than ever. Contact me if you feel your machine needs a service.

Can you collect and return my PC?

Absolutely, If due to your busy schedule you havent got time to come to me or if you do not drive, contact me and i will make arrangements to come to you. If you are leaving your PC with me for work to be done, i can also drop it back to you once the work is finished.
